Privacy

How Port Royal Sounder handles newsletter subscriptions and reader data.

Home › Privacy

Port Royal Sounder respects privacy.

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is stored and used only to send you Port Royal Sounder updates. Your email address will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Email delivery and subscription management are handled by Buttondown, which securely stores subscriber email addresses on behalf of Port Royal Sounder.

Port Royal Sounder does not use advertising trackers or sell subscriber data. Basic analytics provided by Buttondown such as email opens or link clicks may be used to understand what readers find useful and to improve the newsletter.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email.
